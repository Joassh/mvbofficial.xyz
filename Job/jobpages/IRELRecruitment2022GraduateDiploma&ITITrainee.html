Eligibility Criteria:

▪︎ Graduate Trainee (Finance) - CA intermediate or CMA intermediate / Graduate in Commerce from a recognized Indian University with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate for SC candidates and 60% marks in aggregate for others.

▪︎ Graduate Trainee (HR) - Graduate in any discipline from a recognized Indian University with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate for SC candidates and 60% marks in aggregate for others.

▪︎ Diploma Trainee (Technical) - 3 years Diploma in Mining / Chemical / Mechanical / Electrical / Civil / Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from an Institute recognized by AICTE or equivalent with minimum 50% marks in aggregate for SC/ST candidates and 60% marks in aggregate for others.

▪︎ Junior Supervisor (Rajbhasha) - Master‟s Degree from a recognized University in Hindi with English as a compulsory or elective subject or as the medium of examination at the degree level Or

▪︎ Personal Secretary - Graduate in any discipline from a recognized University with English as a compulsory or elective subject or as the medium of examination at the degree level with Typing speed of 40 wpm in English and Stenographic skills. or

▪︎ Tradesman Trainee (ITI) - Must have passed SSC or equivalent Examination from a Board recognized by the State/Central Government with ITI / NAC with two years‟ experience in the relevant Trade/ Discipline in a reputed Industrial Establishment including on the Job Training – Apprenticeship training, if any.

▪︎ Fitter / Electrician - Must have passed SSC or equivalent Examination from a Board recognized by the State/Central Government with ITI/NAC in Attendant Operator (Chemical Plant) or+(plus) 2 Science (Intermediate/Higher Secondary) with Chemistry as one of the subjects & 50% marks in aggregate and with two years‟ experience in the operation of Chemical and allied Process Industry including apprenticeship training, if any.

Application Fee:

▪︎ Non-refundable Application Fee of ₹ 472/- inclusive of GST for UR

▪︎ SC/ST/PwBD/ESM category candidates, Women, and Internal candidates are exempted from payment of the Application Fee

Eligibility Criteria

▪︎ The mode of selection for all the posts would consist of

▪︎ (i) Written Test [First Level Test]

▪︎ (ii) Skill Test / Trade Test / Computer Proficiency Test and Psychometric Test [Second Level Test]as applicable and/or any combination thereof as may be prescribed or decided by the Competent Authority.

▪︎ The written test will be Computer Based Test (CBT) for Post Codes with PC1 to PC5 and Paper Based Test (PBT) for Postcodes with PC6, PC7 & PC8. The candidates fulfilling the eligibility requirements/criteria viz. Age, qualification, experience etc. will be called for a written test (objective type) comprising of Multiple-Choice Questions.
