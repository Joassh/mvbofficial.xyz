Job Description:

▪︎ SAP is hiring for the role of Developer Associate.

Job Responsibilities:

▪︎ Work in accordance with the Agile development models (scrum, lean), which requires you to be self-driven and make decisions independently, as well as to be flexible in order to meet the needs of the team.

▪︎ Work as a developer in a team, taking product development responsibilities for backend development and related processes, methods, and tools for SAP S/4HANA. You will be working on developing legal reports to meet legal regulations for several countries.

▪︎ Write efficient and maintainable code in accordance with standards and naming conventions defined by SAP.

▪︎ Drive topics independently and have object ownership.

▪︎ Play an integral role in the entire software development lifecycle.

▪︎ Be a strong team player who can contribute to the overall success of the team and its deliverables.

▪︎ Effectively communicate with local and remote teams.

Eligibility Criteria:

▪︎ BE/ B.Tech/ ME/ M.Tech MCA or similar

▪︎ 0-3 years of work experience in software development who has knowledge of Agile development in the Scrum environment.

Preferred skill:

▪︎ Proficiency in software design principles and object-oriented design.

▪︎ Working knowledge of classic ABAP and object-oriented ABAP.

▪︎ Working knowledge of Open Data Protocol (OData) and Core Data Services (CDS).

▪︎ Good knowledge of the ABAP programming model for SAP Fiori.

▪︎ Good analytical and problem-solving skills.

▪︎ Working functional or process knowledge of Financials (preferable).

▪︎ Working knowledge of Identity and Access Management (IAM)
