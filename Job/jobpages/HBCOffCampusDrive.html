Job Responsibilities :

▪︎ Works with Global-Customers / IP-providers / Foundries to understand design challenges of cutting-edge SoCs & 3DICs on 7/5/3 nm and creates EDA product specifications.

▪︎ Works with Product development team to get state-of-the-art EDA products developed.

▪︎ Works on Ansys-Seascape platform – Semiconductor Industry’s First and Only True Big-Data design Platform!

▪︎ Works with top Semiconductor companies around the globe to deploy EDA products for solving Power/Signal/Reliability challenges across Chip-Package-System at 7/5/3 nm.

▪︎ Provides expert guidance / consultation to Customers around the globe for solving design challenges.





Eligibility Criteria :

▪︎ B.E / B.Tech: Computer Science, Information Technology

▪︎ BSc: CS/ IT/ PCM/ Statistics

▪︎ Bachelor’s in Computer Science (BCS)

▪︎ MCA: Computer Application

▪︎ Aggregate %: Minimum 60% in SSC (X), HSC (XII), Graduation (UG) and Post- Graduation (PG) [All Semesters]

▪︎ Backlog: NO active backlog(s) at the time of joining.

▪︎ Education Break: Maximum 1 year break in education is allowed (from X/SSC to Highest Qualification)

Preferred skill :

▪︎ Excellent verbal and written communication skills

▪︎ Willing to work in different time zones to support the global team

▪︎ Ready to undergo training programs as required by the organization

▪︎ Self-motivated to achieve excellence with a “can do” attitude and always ready to go beyond and above the limit

▪︎ Ready to quickly learn new in-demand technologies as needed
