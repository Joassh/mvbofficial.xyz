Application Process

▪︎ Date of Written Test is 07-Aug-2022.

▪︎ Please click on “Apply Now” button on this page and fill in your details & submit.

▪︎ Based on the applications received, candidates will be shortlisted for the written test and they will be informed via Email / SMS by 02-Aug-2022.

▪︎ Shortlisted candidates should download the hall ticket from this website.

▪︎ To gain access to the examination venue, the candidate must bring a printout of the hall ticket.

▪︎ Candidate needs to bring any one of Photo ID proof – Aadhar / Driving license / PAN card etc. to exam venue.

▪︎ Candidates must bring their own writing pads & pens. As we provide seats alone & no desks would be available at the exam hall, candidates are advised to bring their own writing pads to assist in comfortable writing.

▪︎ Based on the performance in the exam, selected/wait-listed candidates will be notified via Email / SMS, to attend the 2nd round (Technical & HR round). 2nd round can be in person or virtual.
