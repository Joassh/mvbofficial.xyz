Job Responsibilities :

▪︎ Performs IT functions such as design, analysis, evaluation, testing, debugging, implementation/integration, and operations of new tools, systems.

▪︎ Specify, build, maintain and support technical infrastructure. Analyzes installs, acquires, modifies, and supports operating systems, platforms, databases, or utility software.

▪︎ Focused on configuring, installing, upgrading, monitoring, and scripting existing infrastructure, platforms, and applications to get the desired performance required.

▪︎ Performs high-level root cause analysis for service interruptions and brings services back online once a problem is resolved.

▪︎ Debugs and troubleshoots systems issues by creating patches or re-engineering the system.





Eligibility Criteria :

▪︎ Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science or Information Science

▪︎ CGPA of 8.0 (out of 10) and above

Preferred skill :

▪︎ Excellent written and verbal communication skills. Must be fluent in English.

▪︎ Ability to simply and effectively communicate complex technical concepts to stakeholders

▪︎ Strong problem-solving instincts and abilities

▪︎ Familiar with standard programming languages

▪︎ Knowledge and experience with servers of various operating systems or middleware technologies in an enterprise private/hybrid cloud infrastructure.
